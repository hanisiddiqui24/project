What is Cold?

Cold is the presence of low temperature, typically referring to the winter season. It's characterized by a decrease in warmth, often leading to frosty conditions.

Winter Activities

Embrace the cold with various winter activities like skiing, snowboarding, ice skating, and more! Explore the beauty of winter landscapes and enjoy the thrill of cold-weather sports.
